# QuestHub Master Blueprint

## 1. Core Vision
QuestHub is an automation-first teen talent guild platform where businesses bring tasks, QuestHub distributes them to ranked teen workers, reviews work, protects client relationships, and earns commission.

Core identity:
- Futuristic elite system
- Teen talent arena
- Guild progression engine
- Business trust-first ecosystem

---

## 2. Business Model
Flow:
Business → QuestHub → Teen → QuestHub Review → Client Preview → Payment → Final Delivery

Revenue:
- Founder takes percentage based on rank
- Lower ranks = higher commission
- Higher ranks = lower commission

Rank influence:
F/E = highest cut
A/S = lower cut
SS/SSS = premium trust level

## 4. Rank System
Ranks:
F → E → D → C → B → A → S → SS → SSS

Rank logic based on:
- XP
- Reliability
- Quality
- Trust
- Reviews
- Activity

Special rules:
- S rare
- SS mythical
- SSS near-founder tier

---

## 5. Trial System
Applicants:
- Sign up
- Submit skills
- Enter trial

Trial types:
- Real tasks
- Practice tasks

Measured by:
- Reliability
- Quality
- Communication
- Speed

Duration:
Few hours to one week.

Rejected users can reapply.
